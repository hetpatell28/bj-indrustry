ABOUT US

BJ Industry

We are one of the largest reputed manufacturers of Double wall corrugated pipes in the country. We manufacture corrugated pipes for Cable Protection and Waste Water Management largely used by infrastructural contractors, road contractors and civil engineering companies.

Our pipes are segmented into 2 types: Electrical Conduits ranging from OD 40 mm to 315 OD mm diameter & Sewerage, Drainage Pipes ranging from ID 75 to ID 600 mm diameter.

We at BJ Industry always strive to deliver more than we are committed to deliver. This is the reason why we are most preferred manufacturers of DWC pipes among contractors. Our motto is customer satisfaction, and we constantly seek quality improvement.
