QA & Quality Policy

QA TESTS

We follow the following tests for quality assurance.

  • Density Test
  • Mass Melt Flow Rate Test
  • Ring Flexibility
  • Ring Stiffness
  • Impact Strength
  • Water Tightness
  • Thermal Stability
  • Creep Ratio (Oxidation Induction Test)
